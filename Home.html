Annikedel Company is a Venture Building-as-a-Service platform to help underrepresented founders build products that a lot of people would love, find product-market fit and raise money. We are like Y Combinator for African MSMEs. We want to model an agile and high-growth startup framework in traditional SMEs operational structure to help them scale.

Since inception in 2019, we have offered our startup consultancy services to startups in major cities around the world such as the Silicon Valley, Milton Canada, Lagos Nigeria, among others. Our founders have gone ahead to raise over $1,200,000 in funding.
