Welcome To Annikedel - The Driving Force Behind African Dynamism

At Annikedel, we believe that supporting startup innovation is critical for African Dynamism and the growth of the African continent. Our mission is to help founders build companies that support continental interests in sectors such as financial services, education, culture, agriculture, supply chain, industrials, and manufacturing.

We are committed to identifying and supporting mission-driven and civic-minded founders who are passionate about solving important national and continental problems. These founders often build companies that transcend verticals and business models. The success of these companies supports the flourishing of all Africans.
