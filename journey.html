Our Journey

Annikedel began in 2019 as a startup consulting firm focused on helping early-stage companies grow and scale. We started by offering support services to startups of various shapes and sizes, from small revenue-generating businesses to global-serving companies with annual revenues of up to $100 million.

Our founders collaborated with daring founders to build and scale something from nothing, and we quickly became the go-to support partner for startup innovation in major cities around the world, such as Silicon Valley, Milton Canada, Lagos Nigeria, among others. We were agile operators, community-led, and truly wanted to help.

As we continued to grow, we recognized that there was a need to support startup innovation across the African continent. The African ecosystem faces challenges in achieving sustained prosperity growth while dealing with high population growth and unemployment rates. That"s why we believe that startups, particularly those with a focus on technology and innovation, have the potential to change the narrative and create breakthrough opportunities for individuals and existing businesses, while also being employed by African governments.

We then pivoted towards becoming more than just a venture builder. We became the driving force behind African Dynamism - a movement focused on promoting startup innovation across the African continent. We believe that supporting startup innovation is critical for African Dynamism and the growth of the African continent. Our mission is to help founders build companies that support continental interests in sectors such as financial services, education, culture, agriculture, supply chain, industrials, and manufacturing.
